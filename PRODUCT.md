# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Newser is used by technical readers, engineers, tech leads, and analysts who need a fast operational view of AI, developer-tooling, infrastructure, and security signals.

## Product Purpose

Newser collects technical articles and repositories, filters noise, groups active topics, and presents concise updates for daily monitoring.

## Brand Personality

Focused, technical, calm.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site decoration, heavy gradients, playful dashboards, low-contrast muted text, and visual noise that slows scanning.

## Design Principles

- Keep the interface task-first and scannable.
- Preserve a restrained operational command-center identity.
- Use color for state, selection, and hierarchy, not decoration.
- Make dense information readable on desktop and mobile.
- Keep motion short and tied to state changes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for core text and controls. Respect reduced-motion preferences and keep keyboard focus visible.
